WORKSHOP OBJECTIVES

The workshop is geared towards fostering novel relationships among data providers, users, and beneficiaries of Earth observation data within the CMS4D project. Our objectives include:

  1. Soliciting ideas and proposals for forthcoming events and establishing a sustained communication conduit with suppliers, users, and beneficiaries involved in the project.
  2. Generating and/or collaboratively developing innovative ideas aimed at optimizing the utilization of Earth observation data to address the scientific and management imperatives of Brazil's natural and land resource management agencies.
  3. Identifying requirements and/or crafting educational and cultural materials to bolster the utilization of existing resources for fire management purposes.

EXPECTED GOALS

Outlined below is a list of potential products that may emerge from the seminar and working group sessions. These products will be drafted during the event and subsequently refined based on the outcomes of the group activities:

  1. A concise summary, encompassing actionable ideas leveraging Earth observations to address the scientific and management priorities of land and natural resource management agencies participating in the event.
  2. Contributions towards developing a comprehensive roadmap outlining both current and future project activities. This roadmap will support the creation of products aimed at enhancing fire management efforts.
  3. Identification of potential leaders and collaborators to foster communities of practice around the key themes discussed during the workshop. This will involve establishing active communication channels to facilitate collaboration.
  4. Development of educational materials to be published on the CMS4D project website, aimed at disseminating knowledge and insights generated during the event.
