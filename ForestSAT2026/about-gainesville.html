About Gainesville

Gainesville is a vibrant city with a wide range of activities and attractions for visitors. Known for its friendly atmosphere and rich cultural scene, Gainesville offers plenty of options for everyone to enjoy!

Gainesville is a great place to explore the natural beauty of Florida. With numerous parks, lakes, and nature reserves, you can experience the outdoors through hiking, birdwatching, and wildlife viewing. The city is also renowned for its commitment to preserving and celebrating its natural environment.

Climate

Gainesville enjoys a pleasant climate with warm summers and mild winters. The city averages over 2,800 hours of sunshine and about 140 cm of rain each year. Temperatures in May typically range from 18 - 28ºC. It can get cooler in the evenings, so be sure to bring attire for cooler weather during evening activities.

  • Daily high temperatures in early May range from 84°F to 88°F.
  • Daily low temperatures range from 60°F to 63°F.
  • The average temperature is 73°F and early May rarely brings showers.

Dining

Gainesville has something for everyone. From casual eateries to fine dining, you can find American, Mexican, Italian, Asian, Mediterranean, and many other cuisines. Be sure to try some local favorites and enjoy the vibrant food scene. For a detailed list of dining options in the Gainesville area, check out Visit Gainesville's Delicious Dining page.

Local Transport around Gainesville

For short-distance travel around Gainesville, taxis and rideshare services like Uber and Lyft are readily available. Visit YELP to find Gainesville Taxi Services and read current reviews!

Public Transportation

To use the public bus system (RTS), all-day passes can be purchased directly from the bus driver. Pay fare at first boarding – good for unlimited rides for 24 hours. Please have correct fare amount in US dollars. 24 Hour passes can be purchased for $3.00.

Code of Conduct

We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form. Suggestive statements, sexual innuendo, or offensive remarks are not appropriate during any activity, including during talks, poster sessions, workshops, social functions, after hours parties, via Zoom chat or on Twitter or other online media. Participants asked to stop any harassing behavior are expected to comply immediately. Those violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled without a refund at the discretion of the organizers. If you are being harassed, notice that someone else is being harassed, or have any other concerns about participant behavior, please notify the organizers immediately.

Hotel Reservation Fraud Alert

It is possible you may be contacted by a company claiming to be the official housing bureau for this conference. These companies contact conference exhibitors and attendees, telling them the guest room block is sold out at the host hotel. The company then quotes a rate at another hotel, claiming it is an official room block and that you must reserve through them to get discounted rates. They ask for your credit card information so they can make a reservation for you. DO NOT FALL FOR THIS or you will lose your money. This scam is affecting conferences all over the country. No matter what meeting you attend, always use the hotel reservation links provided on the host organization’s website.
